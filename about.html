About

Producer/ Multi-Instrumentalist Dan Rico blends vintage pop, soul, punk, and rock into a style that's fresh, vibrant, and unique. Drawing inspiration through the ages from the likes of artists like Elvis, Prince, David Bowie, The Ramones, and more, Dan Rico channels soulful songs of love, discord, friendship, and culture. Dan was born and resides in Chicago, IL.
